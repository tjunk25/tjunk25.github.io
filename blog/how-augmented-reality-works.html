How Augmented Reality Works

Augmented Reality is, in a simple way, the fusion between the real world and the virtual world. With it, you can see and interact with virtual objects in the real world.

How Augmented Reality Works

Douglas Souza
Especialista em Marketing Digital Parceiro do Pinmyspot

Imagine that you are on the internet about to buy new furniture for your office, but as usual, there are always a number of variables that we usually take into consideration, among them: "Will it match the environment? "Shopping on the web always has this problem. You did not see. You did not get it. From clothing to home appliances, we want to feel the product, even if it is not for touch.

With Augmented Reality you could pick up this furniture and position it right there in your office. Without leaving the house, just with a touch on your smartphone.

Of course the applications of Augmented Reality are not limited there. There is an unimaginable vastness of possibilities that have not yet been explored, and which will undoubtedly cause a lot of buzz in the next few years. For purposes of understanding, we hope that examples of everyday use can give a dimension of the value that this technology can add to people's lives.

Difference between Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality

These two technologies are often confused, so let's explain a bit the difference between the two and use some examples before going deeper into the practical applicabilities of Augmented Reality.

Virtual reality:

In Virtual Reality we are inserted in the digital environment. The walls, furniture, characters, everything we interact with is digital. It is a complete immersion.

You may have seen some funny videos that have come from people going through comic experience using this technology.

With Virtual Reality you enter the digital world.

Augmented Reality:

As in the example of the furniture we used at the beginning of the text, the virtual object came into the real world. That's the big difference. Parts of the virtual world comes to us.

What is the future of Augmented Reality and what are its applications?

According to Digi-Capital studies, the market for Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality will together be a market of US $ 150 billion.
Much of this amount (US $ 120 billion) will be with Augmented Reality. Part of this is the simplicity of using this technology. Any smartphone camera with an Augmented Reality-based application can play the objects. And with the popularization of smartphones, it is difficult to doubt these projections.
